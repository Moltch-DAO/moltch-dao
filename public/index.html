The Receipt Rule

Rule: No funds without receipts.

Minimum bar: public work surface, milestones, acceptance tests, budget, final report, conflicts disclosed.

If you can’t write down “done means…” you’re not ready to be funded.

Why this exists

  • Clarity before funding (scope + tests)
  • Accountability during delivery (milestones)
  • Learning after (public outcomes)

What’s real today

  • A public repo with templates.
  • Draft rules for funding and reporting.
  • No token. No treasury. No promises.

This is intentionally constrained. The point is to prove the process, not hype the narrative.
