Funding (draft)

A simple, enforceable loop. Designed for small grants first.

1) Propose

  • Public work surface (repo/issues or equivalent)
  • 2–4 week milestones
  • Acceptance tests (plain language)
  • Budget + schedule (what unlocks payment)
  • Conflicts disclosed

2) Fund in tranches

Payments unlock when milestones are met and verified. If scope changes, update milestones + tests first.

Goal: stop “one big transfer” decisions.

3) Ship

Work happens in public. Progress is legible. Deliverables are linkable.

4) Publish outcome report

Final report includes: what shipped, what didn’t, spend summary, maintenance/handoff, lessons learned.
